DigiMuse.ai is your AI-powered career intelligence platform that helps you track your work, develop your skills, and advance your career through intelligent insights and automation.

## What is DigiMuse.ai?

DigiMuse.ai combines the power of artificial intelligence with an intuitive chat interface to help you:
- **Track your work** - Log your daily activities, projects, and achievements
- **Build your skills** - Automatically extract and organize your skills from your work
- **Generate insights** - Get AI-powered career recommendations and reports
- **Manage your career** - Keep everything organized in one intelligent platform

## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Visit the DigiMuse.ai website
2. You'll see a login form on the main screen
3. Click **"Create Account"** below the login form
4. Follow the chat prompts to:
   - Choose a username
   - Create a secure password
   - Enter your invite passkey (provided by your organization or admin)
5. You're all set! You'll be automatically logged in

**Note**: DigiMuse.ai is invite-only. You'll need a valid invite passkey to create an account.

### Signing In

If you already have an account:
1. Enter your username and password in the login form
2. Check "Remember me on this device" to stay logged in for 30 days
3. Click **Sign In**

You can also sign in by:
- Clicking the user icon in the top-right corner
- Selecting "Sign In" from the dropdown menu

## Understanding the Interface

DigiMuse.ai has a clean, modern interface with three main areas:

### 1. Top Bar
- **Brand Logo** (left): Click to return to home
- **Your Profile** (right): Shows your avatar (Gravatar) and username
  - Click to access Dashboard, Settings, and Sign Out options

### 2. Chat Panel (Top)
The chat panel is your main way to interact with DigiMuse.ai:
- Type messages and questions naturally
- The AI assistant responds with helpful information
- Use commands to perform specific actions
- Press **Enter** to send a message
- Press **Shift+Enter** to add a new line

### 3. Canvas (Bottom)
The canvas displays:
- Your dashboard with key statistics
- Forms for entering data
- Detailed views of your work and skills
- Reports and visualizations

### 4. Status Bar (Bottom)
Shows real-time information:
- **Token Count**: How many AI tokens you've used this session
- **Reserve Balance**: Your account's credit balance

## Privacy & Security

Your data is protected:
- **Secure Authentication**: Session-based login with encryption
- **Data Privacy**: Your work logs and skills are private to your account
- **AI Processing**: AI runs locally (Ollama) - your data stays on the server
- **Backup & Recovery**: Regular automated backups
- **HTTPS**: All connections are encrypted (in production)

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Contact your system administrator for password reset assistance.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Yes, contact your administrator to request account deletion.

**Q: How much does it cost?**
A: Check with your organization. DigiMuse.ai uses a reserve-based billing system.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes, you can export your work logs and skills from the dashboard.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes, all user data is private and tenant-isolated. Only you and your organization's admins can access your data.

**Q: What AI model does DigiMuse.ai use?**
A: DigiMuse.ai uses Ollama for local AI processing, ensuring your data stays private and secure.

**Q: How accurate is the AI?**
A: The AI is designed to assist you, but always review and verify its suggestions before using them.
